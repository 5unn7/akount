Guidelines
Anti-Patterns
What NOT to do in the Financial Clarity aesthetic.
✗ DON'T
  • • Heavy drop shadows
  • • Solid white backgrounds in dark mode
  • • Flat gray borders (use rgba white)
  • • 500-level semantic colors on dark
  • • Dense text walls without disclosure
  • • Competing accent colors (orange is primary)
✓ DO
  • • Subtle border + glow for elevation
  • • Glass tiers for all containers
  • • rgba(255,255,255) borders with low alpha
  • • 400-level colors for dark readability
  • • Progressive disclosure, expand on click
  • • Orange primary, everything else supports
